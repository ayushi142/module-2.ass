Freedom from and for

"freedom from"-which is a freedomthat comes from breaking out of 'Psychological slavery' imposed by outside forces such as parents,society,or religion. "freedom for"-a positive freedom that comes from embracing and creating something or an artistic or humanitarian vision.

Epilogue

True freedom is spiritual it has nothing to do with the outside world.It is not political,not economic.It has something to do with your innermost being,which cannot be chained or put into jail.it is that you are free from the past,that you are free from the future.
